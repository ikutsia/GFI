Board of Filmmakers

The Board of Filmmakers, comprised of distinguished representatives from the film industry, appoints the Executive Committee and is responsible for selecting the Institute's President and honorary members.

Additionally, the Board designs a comprehensive three-year action plan. Once approved, this plan is carried out by the Executive Committee, steering and advancing the Institute's initiatives.
